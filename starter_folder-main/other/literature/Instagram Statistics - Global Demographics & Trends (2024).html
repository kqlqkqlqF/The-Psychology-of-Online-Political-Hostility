Instagram Statistics – Global Demographics & Trends (2024)

By Rohit Shewale / January 11, 2024

Since its introduction in 2010, Instagram has skyrocketed to over 2.4 billion active users, making it one of the top 5 social networking platforms globally. If we put it on a scale, a quarter of the world’s active netizens use the platform every month!

Instagram will turn 13 in 2024, and it is only getting younger with more features and improved algorithms!

If you want to plan your marketing strategy for Instagram in 2024, it’s critical to stay up to date on its current state and trends. We have covered everything from Instagram’s demographics to the best time to post on the platform in this post.

About Instagram:

Instagram Statistics 2024 (Top Picks)

  • Instagram currently has over 2.4 billion users, this number is projected to reach 2.5 billion by the end of 2024.
  • Over 500 million users access Instagram every day.
  • 61% of Instagram’s audience is aged between 18 to 34.
  • An average user spends 24 minutes on Instagram every day.
  • 50.7% of Instagram users are males, and 49.3% are females.

Instagram Monthly Active Users (MAUs)

There are currently over 2.4 billion monthly active Instagram users. The platform gained 100 million users in the previous quarter alone.

Considering the growth trend, the number of Instagram MAUs is estimated to reach over 2.5 billion by the end of Q1 2024.

Instagram Daily Active Users (DAUs)

500 million people use Instagram daily.

It’s important to note that this is the official figure released back in 2017; official data is not available on Instagram DAUs since.

During that time, Instagram had 800 million MAUs. That means back in 2017, 62.5% of Instagram’s MAUs were its DAUs

Now that Instagram’s MAUs have crossed the 2.5 billion mark, the number of DAUs might easily be over 1 billion. However, this is just a prediction, let’s wait for the official announcement to roll out.

Some Quick Pointers on Instagram DAUs:

  • With 500 million DAUs, Instagram is ranked second only to Facebook in terms of daily logins.
  • All of the 500 million traffic interact with Instagram stories.
  • Instagram is used by 9.86% of the world’s 5.07 billion Netizens daily.
  • Instagram is used by 10% of the globe’s 4.9 billion active social media users daily.
  • Instagram is used by 6.32% of the world’s population daily.
  • Instagram’s DAUs are 69 million more in numbers than the entire population of South America.

How to Tackle the Issue of Time Zones:

If your followers are from various nations or even the east and west coasts of the United States, this might affect your engagement.

The easiest approach to deal with this is to use Instagram’s insights function, which provides free statistics to all professional account users in the application itself.

To access it, 

  • navigate to your profile page and click the menu icon in the upper right corner.
  • Select ‘insight’ from the list.
  • Select the ‘audience tab’.
  • scroll down to see the ‘Cities and location’ section.

By examining this data, you can determine what time zones the bulk of your users are in, and then arrange your posts around the top engagement periods We’ve shown above! Easy isn’t it?

Instagram Employee strength

There are currently 450 people working at Instagram serving 2 billion monthly users. This is a minuscule figure when compared to parent corporation Meta’s Facebook, which has approximately 58,600 employees serving 2.9 billion users.

In comparison to other successful social media players, Instagram has always managed to operate with a small crew. For e.g., Twitter employs 6,600 people and serves over one-third of Instagram’s MAUs.

Timeline of workforce

  • 2009: Kevin Systrom begins development on Burbn (Instagram) (single employee)
  • 2010: Mike Krieger joined the project, bringing the total number of staff to 2.
  • 2012: The Instagram staff expanded to 13 people, serving 30 million members before being acquired by Meta for half a billion U.S. dollars.
  • 2022: Instagram has 450 employees serving Over 2 Billion active users.

Trends To Lookout For In 2024

Change is the only constant on Instagram! If you want your presence to be felt by the majority of the audience, you need to stay up-to-date with the latest trends. As we wind up 2023 and enter 2024, we have mentioned some crucial Instagram trends to look into and implement into your campaign to make it big!

Trend #1: Reels, Reels, and more Reels.

Short-form video content is the eye-catcher on almost all social media platforms. Instagram reels, which were launched back in 2019 as the answer to TikToks, are spreading like wildfire. As a matter of fact, every Instagram user interacts with reels when they are on the platform. Check our complete guide on Instagram Reels here.

Trend #2: Paid advertising can be a way!

With the ever-changing Instagram algorithm, it is almost impossible to maintain a good organic reach on the platform. Harness the power of Instagram ads and get your business/brand in front of millions of eyeballs. A survey by Data Reportal reveals that Instagram can reach 1.44 billion people through ads.

Trend #3: Collaborations!

One more great way to get extra reach is to post collaborative posts with accounts with higher engagement.

Trend #4: Dance and Meme Revolution.

One-third of Instagram’s young users are “eager to see more dancing challenges in 2022,” according to the social media platform’s own 2022 trend report. Talking about memes, it has always been there, isn’t it? Make use of these and grow your account to the next level.
